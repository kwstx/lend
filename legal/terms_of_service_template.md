# Terms of Service: Lend Financial Service (Draft)

*Note: This is a community-sourced template for use in the Lend project. This is not legal advice and must be reviewed by qualified legal counsel in each operating jurisdiction.*

## 1. Credit Terms
Lend provides dynamic credit advances based on real-time revenue analysis of Customer's business accounts. Each advance is a purchase of future receivables at a discount, or a loan, as defined in the specific Funding Agreement provided at the time of each offer.

## 2. Real-Time Data Access
By using the service, Customer grants Lend permission to access real-time financial data via third-party providers (e.g., Stripe, Plaid). Customer agrees that this access is a prerequisite for continued credit eligibility.

## 3. Repayment Policy
Repayments are processed automatically as a percentage of Incoming Revenue. If the automated repayment falls below a certain threshold due to technical issues, Lend reserves the right to manually reconcile and debit the Customer's primary bank account for the outstanding obligation.

## 4. Compliance and Verification
All Customers must undergo manual identity (KYC) and business (KYB) verification. Failure to provide accurate registration documents or tax identification will result in immediate suspension of funding capacity.

## 5. Termination and Default
Lend may freeze funding ("Kill Switch") at any time for reasons including, but not limited to:
- Suspected fraud or data manipulation.
- Breach of financial stability thresholds.
- Regulatory changes or global risk escalation.

## 6. Jurisdiction
These terms are governed by the laws of the jurisdiction where the Lend parent entity is registered.
